Announcement

In accordance with the latest New York State and Albany County reopening phase the Ravena Coeymans Historical Society is once again open to visitors. All visitors are required to wear masks and must sign-in upon entering the museum. We ask that all visitors maintain social distancing while in the museum. It is still suggested that if you are visiting to do any type of research that you either e-mail curator@coeymanshistory.org for an appointment (Monday & Thursday afternoons only) or call 518-936-4397 and leave a message and someone will get back to you. If telephoning please call at least one week prior to your visit.

Thank you for your understanding.
